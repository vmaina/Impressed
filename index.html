Patient Resources

A searchable database of no- or low-cost resources for cancer patients and caregivers provided either online or located in Washington, Oregon, Idaho, Montana, Wyoming and Alaska. You will also find support groups or summer camps, transportation or lodging options to be used during treatment, or simply learn more about a cancer type. Interactive features allow for filtering by resource type or cancer focus, searching by key words, scrolling through lists of online and location based resources.
